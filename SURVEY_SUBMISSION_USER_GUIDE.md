# Survey Submission & Approval - Quick Start Guide

## For Survey Users

### How to Submit a Survey for Approval

1. **Complete Your Survey**
   - Fill in all required items for all locations
   - Upload photos where needed
   - Mark each location as complete

2. **Submit for Review**
   - Click the "Preview & Submit" button (green button in header)
   - Review all your entries in the preview modal
   - Click "Submit Survey for Approval" (blue button)
   - Confirm the submission in the dialog

3. **What Happens Next**
   - Your survey is locked (cannot edit)
   - Your supervisor receives an email notification
   - You can track status at: `/SurveySubmission/MySubmissions`

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I edit a survey after submitting?**
A: No, it's locked. If rejected, it will unlock for editing.

**Q: How do I know if my supervisor reviewed my survey?**
A: You'll receive an email and status updates on "My Submissions" page.

**Q: Can I cancel a submission?**
A: Contact your supervisor - they can reject it to unlock for editing.

**Q: What if I don't receive the email?**
A: Check your spam folder. Also check the "My Submissions" page for status.

**Q: How long does review take?**
A: Depends on your supervisor's availability. Check with them if urgent.
